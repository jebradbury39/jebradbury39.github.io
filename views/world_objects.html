World Objects

      Having a world and collision detection isn't much fun without a way to explore the terrain
      and test the collisions. For this, I wanted a way to spawn and manipulate arbitrary objects
      in a manner similar to using Unity or another game engine.
      Enter the WorldObject class:
   




      To control the nearest ControllableObject, just press 'c'. To exit, press 'x'. Further commands
      may be available depending on the type of object.
