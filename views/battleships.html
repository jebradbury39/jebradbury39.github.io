Battleships

      Battleships may be spawned using 'b', but only if the user is positioned over water. The ship
      can be moved in the traditional (and entirely unrealistic) method of WASD. However, when in
      control of a battleship, the user is actually given control of one of the four turrets
      on the ship (each turret is its own ControllableObject). The turret may be rotated using 'q'
      and 'e', the guns elevated with 'r' and 't', and fired with spacebar (two bombs are fired).
      The user can switch between turrets by pressing 'c'. Pressing 'x' will still exit the entire
      ship.
